Calorie Calculator

The calorie calculator can be used to estimate the number of calories a person should consume each day based on the goal of losing weight, gaining weight, or maintaining weight.

Activity levels:

minimal activity level - sedentary work that does not require significant physical exertion;

low activity level - intense exercise for at least 20 minutes one to three times a week. This could be cycling, jogging, basketball, swimming, ice skating, etc. Or busy lifestyle that requires frequent walking;

mild activity level - intense training for at least 30-60 minutes three to four times a week;

heavy activity - intense exercise and sports 5-7 days a week;

extreme level - includes extremely active and / or very energy-intensive activities: sports with an almost daily schedule and several workouts during the day.
